## 2. Environmental Conditions

* [ ] **Visibility**: Clear line of sight between the operator and the signal person.

## 3. Exclusion Zone & Personnel
* [ ] **Safety Perimeter**: Established to prevent any worker from standing under the load.
* [ ] **Communication**: Radio or hand signals confirmed between all team members.

* [ ] **Temporary Bracing**: Installed immediately after placement before releasing the crane hooks.
